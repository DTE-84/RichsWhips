Before posting a single video, spend 2-3 days studying what's already working. AI scraping tools pull public data from top automotive creators so you can reverse-engineer their success without guessing.

Use Apify's TikTok Scraper to extract transcripts from the top 50 trending automotive videos. Feed them into Claude or ChatGPT with the prompt: "What do the first 5 seconds of the top 20 have in common?"

What to Post
CONTENT TYPES THAT CONVERT
🎬
The "Honest Walk-Around"
No script, no salesiness. Just him walking around a fresh lot arrival talking about what he genuinely likes about it. Authenticity is the algorithm's favorite thing right now.
🔥 Highest organic reach
📆
Day in the Life
Show the real job. Pre-delivery inspection, coffee before the lot opens, a delivery moment (with customer permission). Makes him relatable, not a stereotype.
📈 Best for follows
🤔
Myth Busting
"Here's what dealers actually make on a new car." "No, we don't hide fees on Toyotas." Radical transparency = massive shares from people who never thought a car salesman would say that.
♻️ Highest share rate
💰
Deal Breakdowns
"Here's what a $0 down RAV4 Hybrid actually looks like monthly." Show the math. Buyers are scared of math — be the guy who demystifies it. Instant credibility.
💬 High comment volume
🏆
Social Proof Moments
Film the delivery. Get the customer to say one sentence on camera. "Just picked up my Tacoma from [name], smoothest buying experience ever." That's a 15-second closer.
🎯 Direct conversion
🛻
Toyota-Specific Cult Content
4Runner owners are a religion. Tacoma owners are a religion. Make a video just for them. "Every 4Runner that came through the lot this week" gets saved by thousands of Toyota loyalists.
🔖 Massive save rate

08
90-Day Launch
PHASE ROLLOUT PLAN
1
Research & Setup
Week 1–2
  • Set up Apify + Octoparse, scrape top 10 automotive TikTok accounts
  • Feed transcripts into Claude, identify top 5 hook styles and content themes
  • Create TikTok, YouTube, and Facebook accounts with unified branding
  • Install CapCut, practice editing one test video (don't post yet)
  • Set up Publer for cross-platform scheduling
2
Soft Launch — Content Testing
Week 3–5
  • Post 4x/week — 2 walk-arounds, 1 tip video, 1 personality/day-in-life
  • Test 3 different hook styles, track which gets highest retention
  • Engage with every comment within 2 hours of posting
  • Study which video at Day 21 has highest saves-to-views ratio — double down on that format
3
Scale & Local Presence
Week 6–10
  • Introduce location hashtags (#QuincyIL, #AdamsCounty, #WestIllinois)
  • Start filming customer delivery moments (consent form = 30 seconds)
  • Add a "DM me your budget" CTA at end of deal breakdown videos
  • Set up n8n automation to pull weekly competitor intelligence briefing
  • Explore TikTok Automotive Inventory Ads (AIAs) — launching 2026
4
Monetize the Audience
Week 11–13
  • Convert social followers into cold email/SMS list via "DM for inventory alerts"
  • Tie social content into existing cold call/email campaigns for warm follow-up
  • Review 90-day analytics, double down on top 3 performing content types
  • Evaluate paid TikTok/Facebook boost on top 3 organic performers
09
Stay Clean
ETHICAL & LEGAL GUARDRAILS
⚖️
Scrape public data only. All tools listed pull publicly available info (views, likes, captions, public video metadata). Never attempt to scrape private accounts or bypass login walls — legal exposure isn't worth it.
🐢
Rate-limit your scraping. Don't pull thousands of pages per minute. Configure Apify/Octoparse with delays between requests to avoid IP blocks and TOS flags. Slow and steady wins.
📋
Dealership social media policy. Shottenkirk doesn't allow individual websites — confirm their social media policy before posting anything that mentions the dealership name, inventory prices, or specific deal structures. Frame it as "personal brand" if needed.
📸
Customer consent for video. Always get verbal (or written) consent before filming customers or using their likeness. A simple thumbs-up on camera and "is it cool if I post this?" counts.
